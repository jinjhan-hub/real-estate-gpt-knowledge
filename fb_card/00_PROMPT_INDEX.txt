1. 01_PROPERTY_DATA_EXTRACTION.txt
2. 02_BUSINESS_CARD_EXTRACTION.txt
3. 03_FB_CARD_CONTENT_RULES.txt
4. 04_IMAGE_STYLE_LIBRARY.txt
5. 05_IMAGE_PROMPT_TEMPLATE.txt

7. 07_AD_COMPLIANCE_CHECK.txt

- FB 長文文案模板
- IG 文案規則
- Threads 文案規則
- 開頭句庫
- CTA 長文模板

1. 01_PROPERTY_DATA_EXTRACTION.txt
2. 02_BUSINESS_CARD_EXTRACTION.txt

4. 07_AD_COMPLIANCE_CHECK.txt
